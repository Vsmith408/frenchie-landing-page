About Frenchies

French bulldogs are great dogs! They do well in an apartment; however, do not like being alone. They're super friendly, are great around kids and other dogs. They usually weigh about 15-30 pounds and are about one foot tall.

French bulldogs live approximately 11-14 years. They're very easy to train but have a tendency to chew on things. Although being a high energy breed, they do not like to go for long walks or runs .
